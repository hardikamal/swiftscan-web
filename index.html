Built for the Real World

ScanCrypt was built out of frustration. Document apps were either expensive, bloated, or cloud-dependent. We wanted something different — fast, private, and powerful.

Every feature in ScanCrypt runs directly on your device. No uploads. No subscriptions required to get started. No watching your files disappear into someone else's server.

CryptonStudio is a small, focused team obsessed with building tools that actually work the way you expect — instantly and reliably. ScanCrypt is our flagship commitment to that standard.

Privacy-first. Performance-first. You-first.
